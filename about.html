About Us

Overview

MD URBAN is an online delivery system and platform that allows its user create an online profile which allows us the service provider deliver health, medical and insurance related services directly to the end user In-home, In-office and In-community.
The key components of MD URBAN are:

  1. Health and Medical services. 
  2. Health Insurance Services.
  3. Access to foreign medical specialists around the world.
  4. E-commerce.


Mission & Vision

VISION: To build a self-sustainable public health and preventive health care delivery system based on cultural, socio-economical and religious principles.
MISSION: To promote and develop a self-sustainable , cultural and religious sensitive health and welness preventive care ,medical care, and insurance delivery system across Nigeria and Africa at Large.

Goals & Objectives

GOAL: To promote and facilitate the development of online, door-to-door , Business-to-Business, and onsite diagonistic centers for the prevention of lifestyle modifiable diseases, maternal and infant mortality, prevention of HIV/AIDS, Malaria and Tuberculosis in Nigeria and Africa.
The specific objectives are:

  1. To facilitate onsite mobile clinics/centers across major cities in Nigeria.
  2. To assess and assure patients and clients via online delivery system.
  3. To increase access to diagnostics mobile centres in Nigeria, taking into account religious    and gender sensitive issues in Africa.
  4. To set standards for mobile/Online medical service delivery that cuts across lifestyle medicine, community medicine and sustainable development.
