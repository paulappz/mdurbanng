Services

Experience wellness services at its best.

Just for you

Fill a health form on or before you arrive, providing information on your current activities: eating habits, sleep patterns, and overall wellness goals. Starting with this level of personalization ensures that you will receive a customized experience and leave with an individualized or corporate plan to help you achieve your wellness goals.

Access to Wellness Record

Support your life-long wellness journey with continued access to your Wellness Assessment.

MDUrban Certified Wellness Coach

Engage your personal coach in one-on-one sessions where you are able to express your goals and challenges in confidentiality. Learn techniques and tips in the areas of meditation, nutrition, physical activity, relaxation, and more.

Advanced Assessment

Receive a personal wellness assessment with a MDUrban range of interpretation, delivered to you by trusted experts. If your assessment does reveal any urgent or emerging medical concerns, we will coordinate your care with appropriate MDUrban specialists.
  • Biometric Evaluation
  • Body Composition Scan
  • Blood Test
  • Lipid Profile
  • Movement Assessment
  • Strength Assessment

Expert Guidance

MDUrban experts will take the time to listen to and answer your questions, assuring that you gain personalized knowledge and equipments to apply to your health and well-being.

Personalized Wellness Plan

Leave with a detailed analysis of your health and strategies to improve your well-being. Engage with your personalized health and wellness coach to create a personal nutrition plan and fitness plan tailored to your lifestyle. We offer Corporate and individual therapeutic massage that could come as an in-house service rendered by our mobile wellness team. This personalized, easy-to-read plan, developed by MDUrban physicians and experts, will empower you to succeed.

Progressive Support

Maintain a strong connection to your MDUrban team who will continue to support you on your journey when you return home. Your team will help you stay motivated and on track.
